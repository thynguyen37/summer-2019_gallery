My Viet-Nam Trip

My first trip to VietNam was in 2017 and I can remember it vividly.

It had been more than 30 years since I was a little boy living there. There are so many new things that I have to get used to: like how to cross the street since there are so many scooters and they just zig zag around you haphazardly.

The foods were delicious and considerably cheaper than here in the States. The weather is another: humid and hot. But, I can't wait to go back and visit again, despite the constant heat.
